Chicketh

The chicketh is a unique chimera, i.e. composite animal, that results from combining a rooster, waterbuck and a cat.

What They Eat

Chickeths are carnivores whose typical prey are small- to medium-size animals, such as:

  • Gazelles
  • Hares
  • Warthogs
  • Birds
Want to Read More About Chimeras?

Animal Hybrids (or colloquially "Chimeras") should not be confused with genetic chimeras, such as that between sheep and goat known as the geep.
